Daily Photograph 17th May 2024

Liverpool waterfront with the British Antarctic research vessel, the Sir Richard Attenborough. It was taken from the Mersey ferry, enroute from Seacombe to the Pier Head, in the summer of 2021.

It’s a photo of Liverpool today, because my wife and I are off to Liverpool for the weekend.
